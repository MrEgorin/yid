    About Us

    YID is a young and ambitious biotechnology company founded by a team of passionate students from Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Institute of High Technologies, Department of Bioinformatics and Molecular Biotechnology. Our goal is to push the boundaries of modern science and create innovative solutions for global challenges in healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability.

    We believe that the future of biotechnology lies in interdisciplinary collaboration, cutting-edge research, and the application of advanced technologies such as CRISPR-RNA engineering, artificial intelligence, and omics data analysis.

    Our Mission

    At YID, our mission is to revolutionize the biotechnology industry by developing groundbreaking solutions that improve human health, enhance agricultural productivity, and protect our planet's ecosystems. We strive to make science accessible, ethical, and impactful.

    "Science is not just about discovery; it's about making a difference."

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to become a leading force in the global biotechnology landscape, driving innovation and fostering collaboration between academia, industry, and society. We aim to create a world where scientific breakthroughs translate into tangible benefits for humanity.

    Why Choose YID?

    • Innovative Approach: We leverage the latest advancements in biotechnology and bioinformatics to deliver cutting-edge solutions.
    • Expertise: Our team combines deep scientific knowledge with practical experience in drug design, protein engineering, and genomics.
    • Passion: We are driven by a passion for science and a commitment to making a positive impact on the world.
    • Sustainability: We prioritize environmentally friendly practices and strive to minimize our ecological footprint.
